HOW ARE AIRTs CREATED?

The AI find patterns within the database and develops its own rules for how to represent.
These rules are not designed by humans, the AI learns from the data on which the AI was trained. AIRTs will never completely mimic real paintings.



Once the AI has completed the creation of a new AIRT it will assign a rarity to it (Common, Rare or Very Rare) and generete a random name to the final AIRT.
*We want as less human iteraction in the process, to make a genuine AI generated work of art.

There is a limit cap of 75 AIRTs for each category, no more NFTs will be minted after reaching the cap. All AIRTs are 1 of 1 unique pieces.
