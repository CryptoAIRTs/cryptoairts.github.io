WHAT IS AIRT


AIRT is a living digital painting. A trained AI algorithm analyses a database of old master paintings and creates a sequence of new paintings. Each alive painting (AIRT) is a unique 1 of 1 NFT validated token that can be collected and sold on the Ethereum blockchain.

There is a limited number of AIRTs minted, each one is categorized by its rarity: Common, Rare and Very Rare.



(Categories like landscape or abstract might be available in the future)
